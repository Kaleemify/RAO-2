Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our ambulance services

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Our average response time is under 8 minutes for central Karachi areas. We have strategically positioned ambulances across the city to ensure rapid response. Response times may vary based on location, traffic conditions, and time of day.

Do you provide services 24/7?

Yes, Rao Ambulance Services operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all holidays. Our dispatch center and medical teams are always ready to respond to emergency calls at any time.

What medical equipment do your ambulances carry?

Our ambulances are equipped with advanced life support equipment including defibrillators, ventilators, cardiac monitors, IV pumps, oxygen systems, emergency medications, and trauma kits. All equipment is regularly maintained and calibrated.

Are your staff medically trained?

Yes, all our medical staff are certified paramedics and EMTs with extensive training in emergency medical care. They hold current certifications in CPR, ACLS, PALS, and participate in ongoing education programs to maintain their skills.

Do you accept insurance?

We provide detailed receipts and documentation that can be submitted to insurance companies for reimbursement. We also assist with insurance paperwork and can coordinate directly with some insurance providers. Please contact us to discuss your specific insurance coverage.

Can I schedule non-emergency transport in advance?

Absolutely! We offer scheduled medical transport for dialysis appointments, chemotherapy, routine medical visits, and hospital discharges. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability.
